## Protocol

- [Cashu](https://cashu.space) - Electronic cash for payments online, in person, and around the worlds

## Wallets

- [Cashu Nutshell](https://github.com/cashubtc/nutshell) - Chaumian ecash wallet and mint for Bitcoin
- [Cashu.me](https://wallet.cashu.me) - free and open-source Bitcoin wallet that uses ecash to keep your funds secure and private
- [Harbor](https://harbor.cash) - ecash desktop wallet for better bitcoin privacy
- [Minibits](https://www.minibits.cash) - Bitcoin Lightning and ecash wallet that enables instant, low-cost, and private value transfers
- [Nutstash wallet](https://www.nutstash.app) - Cashu Wallet with multi-mint and send-to-nostr-key functionality
- [Sovran](https://sovran.money/en/) - privacy-focused ecash & lightning wallet that prioritizes simplicity and rich social experiences
